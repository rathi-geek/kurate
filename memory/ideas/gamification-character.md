# Gamification: Growing Character as You Curate

## Idea
Grow a character (or visual) as users save/spend time in the app.

## Discussion Points (saved 2026-03-12)

1. **Fit with Kurate's identity** — Kurate is positioned around intellectual curation and "proof of knowledge." A growing character feels more playful/casual. If it represents the user's *knowledge graph* or *reading depth* rather than just raw saves, it could align well.

2. **Metric matters** — Rewarding *saving* quantity incentivises hoarding. Rewarding *reading*, *sharing*, *rating*, or *having your saves discovered by others* aligns better with the app's value prop.

3. **Possible angle** — Rather than a character, a growing visual *constellation* or *knowledge tree* branching by topic could be more on-brand and feel prestigious rather than juvenile.

## Open Question
What is the "spending" mechanic — time spent reading, number of saves, something else?
